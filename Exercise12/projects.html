  1. Moonlancer: Jump

    A mobile game releasing on Android Spring 2019
    Jump from rock to rock trying to avoid falling back to the ground. Your jetpack will save you, but only if you have the fuel!

    The game is set on a distant moon where the hero must jump back into space to rendevous with his space ship. With infinite distance to cover, the only limit to your score is your own ability.



    Placed first in USF Sarasota-Manatee's Undergrad showcase for Poster Presentations in Information Technology Spring 2018. This project is being completed as part of my USF capstone course: "Senior Project for Information Technology".
